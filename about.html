story of
coffee bean

Amazing Drinks, Made by Amazing People for Good People Seeking Great Company in a Fun Space.
Welcome to the official website for the Coffee Bean in Sri Lanka. Our amazing staff strive to carefully craft our drinks and food so you can experience happiness in every sip or bite. Walk in, order, collect and pull up a chair at any of our locations and be a part of a movement that exudes a fun, relaxing ambience for great company fuelled by delicious coffees and food. From our beginnings in England, we have been spreading the good vibes and passion throughout the world. Have a look at our diverse menus or browse our outlet finder to see which of our trendy coffee houses is closest to you. What are you waiting for? The Bean isn't going to order itself. Smiles are free with every order.
